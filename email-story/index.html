Our Story

- Nov. 2015

Matt walks out of a coffee shop after buying a coffee and a granola bar, costing him $6 and 25 mintues.

He asks himself, "why isn't there a healthy, convenient, and inexpensive way to get energy that actually makes me feel good?"

- Apr. 2016

Matt, Bennett, and André make the first Verb Bars in dorm room kitchens.

As they hand them out to classmates, they realize they are onto something: people need energy that doesn't just make them feel good, they need energy they can also feel good about.

- Sep. 2016

The Verb team starts selling handmade Verb Bars that they make at night in a local bakery.

They work all night and deliver boxes of Verb Bars in between classes. They can't keep up with demand - their fellow college students can't get enough.

- Apr. 2017

Verb Energy is launched.

They make Verb Bars at scale, build their brand, fire up a website, and bring Verb to the world. It's a hit - people from all walks of life fall in love with Verb.

- Today

The Verb team is so excited to introduce you to Verb today.
