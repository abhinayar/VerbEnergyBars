Frequently Asked
Questions

  • How can I get Verb Bars in my store? Shoot Bennett an email at bennett@verbenergybar.com, he'll take care of it.
  • How does the amount of caffeine in a Verb Bar compare to suggested amounts? The FDA recommends up to 400mg of caffeine per day. Each Verb Bar has 100mg of caffeine, the same amount as a cup of coffee.
  • Why do you use caffeine from green tea in Verb Bars? We’ve chosen organic green tea because it’s healthy, clean, and provides a long-lasting energy boost. Caffeine from tea is absorbed over a longer period of time than that from other sources. That’s why coffee drinkers usually report feeling more focused and energized after a Verb Bar than after coffee.
  • What if I order Verb Bars and don’t like them? Send us an email at team@verbenergybar.com. We’ll provide a full refund.
  • What’s a Verb subscription? Many of our customers were having one or more Verb Bars each day, so we decided to offer a service that ships bars to your door every month without the need to re-order. If you want 10 bars per month, order here. If you want a bigger subscription, send us an email at team@verbenergybar.com and we'll get you set up.
  • Do you do volume discounts? Can I get Verb bars for my team, group, or company? Absolutely! We love doing big orders, and big orders get big discounts. Get in touch with us at team@verbenergybar.com.
  • How do I get a subscription for more than one box a month? Add a monthly subscription to your shopping cart, then open your cart. You'll be able to change the quantity there.
  • I want to start a company. Do you have any advice? Absolutely! We'll help any way we can. About Us

We founded Verb Energy, Inc. because we were tired of making compromises with our energy. We couldn't find a healthy and sustainable energy source that could keep up with the lives we wanted to live, so we started making one ourselves. After months of test recipes and ingredient research, we achieved what we set out to: a bar that doesn't just feel good, but one you can feel good about. Now Verb Bars are shipped across the country.

Our Mission

We make good energy.

We believe the world is a better place when nothing holds people back from enjoying what they love. That means making the bar that’s best for you, best for the community, and best for the environment. We source responsibly grown organic ingredients, donate a portion of profits to worthy causes, and have made the commitment to be carbon neutral by 2020.
